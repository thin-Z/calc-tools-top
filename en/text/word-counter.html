Online Word Counter & Keyword Density Analyzer

Our free word counter and keyword density analyzer helps writers, editors, students, and SEO professionals quickly understand their text metrics.

Word Count Mode

Quickly count total characters, Chinese characters, English words, paragraphs, sentences, lines, visible characters, digits, and punctuation marks in real time.

Keyword Density Analysis

For English text, words are split by boundaries and common stop words are automatically filtered out for accurate results. For Chinese text, each character is analyzed individually. The top 20 keywords are displayed sorted by frequency with density percentage.

Privacy First

All text processing is performed locally in your browser. Your content never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy.
