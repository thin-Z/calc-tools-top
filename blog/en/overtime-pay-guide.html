Overtime Pay Calculation: Complete Guide

Overtime pay rates depend on when the extra work is performed.

Overtime Rates

Formula

1. Daily wage = Monthly salary / 21.75
2. Hourly wage = Daily wage / 8
3. Overtime pay = Hourly wage x Hours x Rate
