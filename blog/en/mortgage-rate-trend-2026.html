2026 Mortgage Rate Trends

2026 has seen several adjustments in mortgage rates, significantly impacting both homebuyers and existing borrowers. This article covers the latest policies and future trends.

Current Mortgage Rates in 2026

As of mid-2026, first-home mortgage rates are at 3.0%-3.5% (floating above LPR), and second-home rates at 3.5%-4.0%. These are among the lowest levels in the past decade.

Key Factors Affecting Mortgage Rates

LPR (Loan Prime Rate)

The 5-year LPR has remained stable in 2026. Significant short-term fluctuations are unlikely.

City-Specific Policies

First-tier cities generally have higher rates. Some cities offer additional discounts for first-home buyers.

Personal Qualifications

Credit history, income level, and down payment ratio all affect your final approved rate. Maintain a good credit record.

Outlook

Mortgage rates are expected to remain stable through late 2026. Current rates are at historic lows, making it a favorable time for qualified buyers.
