Housing Fund Calculator Guide

The housing fund loan offers lower interest rates, making it the preferred choice for home buyers. This calculator helps you estimate monthly payments and total interest quickly.

Equal Installment vs Equal Principal

Equal installment: fixed monthly payment, suitable for stable income. Equal principal: decreasing monthly payment, lower total interest, suitable for those with higher early-stage repayment capacity.
