UUID & Password Generator

This tool offers two functions: UUID v4 batch generation and customizable password generation. UUIDs are ideal for database primary keys, session IDs, and distributed system identifiers. The password uuid-generator lets you configure character types and length, and evaluates password strength.

Password Strength Levels

  • Very Strong — 16+ characters with all 4 character types
  • Strong — 12-15 characters with at least 3 types
  • Fair — 8-11 characters with at least 2 types
  • Weak — Less than 8 characters or only 1 type
