BMI Calculator Guide

BMI (Body Mass Index) is an internationally recognized measure of body fat based on height and weight. It applies to most adults aged 18-65.

BMI Reference Range

Underweight: < 18.5 | Normal: 18.5-24.9 | Overweight: 25-29.9 | Obese: $$ 30

Note: BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic test. Combine with other metrics for a complete health assessment.
