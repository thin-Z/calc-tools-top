Housing Fund Loan Guide 2026

Housing fund loans offer lower interest rates, making them the preferred choice for home buyers. But the maximum loan amount varies by individual circumstances.

Factors Affecting Loan Amount

  • Contribution Years: Longer = higher limit
  • Account Balance: Typically 10-15x of balance
  • Monthly Contribution: Higher = stronger repayment ability
