Car Loan Calculator: Understanding Payments

Understanding car loans helps you make better financing decisions.

Key Parameters

  • Car price: Total vehicle cost
  • Down payment: 20-50% of price
  • Interest rate: 2.5-7% new, 5-12% used
  • Loan term: 1-7 years

Example

$30,000 car, 20% down ($6,000), loan $24,000 at 5% for 5yr: $453/mo, total interest $3,180.

Tips

1. Bigger down payment = less interest
2. Shorter term = much less interest
3. Compare 3+ lenders
4. Check for prepayment penalties
