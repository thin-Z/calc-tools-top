What is a Normal BMI? Complete Guide

BMI (Body Mass Index) is an internationally recognized measure of body fat based on height and weight. But what's a normal BMI?

Normal BMI Range

According to WHO standards:

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5 ≤ BMI < 25
  • Overweight: 25 ≤ BMI < 30
  • Obese: BMI ≥ 30

BMI Limitations

BMI cannot distinguish muscle from fat. Athletes may have high BMI but low body fat. Combine with body fat percentage and waist circumference for better assessment.
