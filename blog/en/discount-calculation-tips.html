How to Calculate Discounts: Formula and Tips

Understanding discount calculations helps you save money while shopping.

Discount Formula

Sale price = Original price x Discount rate

20% off = multiply by 0.8. 25% off = multiply by 0.75. 50% off = multiply by 0.5.

Savings = Original price - Sale price

Discount Types

Percentage off: Simple, direct percentage reduction. BOGO: Buy one get one free = 50% off on two items. Coupon: Fixed amount off, like $10 off $50.
